introduction

Nishat Ice cream parlour began in 2002 when industry veterans having 20-25 years of experiance in the ice cream industry decided to quit their MNC jobs and brands with their passion. They saw a market oppartunity which no one else did. They believed that flavours had feelings and that taste buds can party.


And also began the Nishat Ice cream parlour journey in a modest 2000 square foot factory. But fueled by the founder's passion expertise, Nishat ice cream quickly evolved to become one of the leading brands in india.


Nishat Ice Cream is currently present across South India , Maharashtra and Odisha.


Infrastructure

Nishat Ice Cream parlour manufractures and supplies 150+ products across a variety of cups, cones, stick, tubs and other special with over 30 flavours. All products arre made in ultra-modern facilities built to international standards and company has state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in karnataka, spread over 2 lakh square feet and have productive capacity of 2 lakh liters per day. The company also has one of the largest candy nad cone making capacity in india.

The entire manufacturing proces is ISO compliant (ISO-22000-2018(FSMS)). The facilities are supervised day by day an expert team of dairy technologists, Food technologists and microbiologists.
